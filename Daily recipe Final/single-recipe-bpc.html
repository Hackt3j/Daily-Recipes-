Banana Pancakes

Banana Pancakes Recipe: Fluffy pancakes with bananas and a hint of vanilla essence. These banana pancakes are a perfect breakfast option.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

10 mins.

serving

4

easy

Tags : Desserts Sweets Vegetable soup

how to make Banana Pancakes

step 1

Whisk egg in a bowl and add butter, honey and vanilla essence to it. Whisk well.

step 2

Add mashed banana to it. Mix well.

step 3

Now sift flour and baking powder to the mixture. Mix well to not let any lumps form.

step 4

Keep adding milk to the mixture while whisking until a smooth paste.

step 5

Now heat a pan and pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake.

step 6

Cook until pancakes are golden brown on both sides.

step 7

Serve hot with sliced banana slices on top.

ingredients of Banana Pancakes

1 Cup All-purpose flour or maida

4 tsp White sugar

1/2 tsp Baking powder

A pinch of Salt

1 Egg

1 1/2 cup Milk

25 ml Vegetable oil

1 Banana, mashed

1/2 tsp Vanilla essence

1 tbsp Honey

1 tsp Butter
