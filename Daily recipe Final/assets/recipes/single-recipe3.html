Mixed Vegetable Soup:

Mixed Vegetable Soup: An ideal way to add vegetables in a diabetic meal, especially for the elderly. An ideal mid meal snack, this soup recipe is specially curated by Ms. Priyam Naik, Officer Dietetics, Saifee Hospital, this mixed vegetable soup has a host of healthy veggies like carrots, french beans, tomatoes and peas that ensures a nutritious, delicious and a wholesome meal.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

35 min.

serving

1

Easy

Tags : Mixed Vegetable Soup Method

how to make Mixed Vegetable Soup

step 1

Cook all the above mentioned vegetables in a pressure cooker with 2 cups water.

step 2

After it is steamed thoroughly, blend it in a blender.

step 3

Strain with a coarse strainer.

step 4

Add the tempering of oil and curry leaves, salt to taste, jeera powder and pepper powder.

step 5

Serve hot.

ingredients of Mixed Vegetable Soup

3 Cups mix vegetables (tomatoes, carrot, peas, tendli, french beans)

to taste salt

1/2 tsp cumin powder

1/2 tsp black pepper powder

For tempering:

1 tsp oil

Few curry leaves

tools

Hand Blender

Large Heavy Pot With Lid

Measuring Spoons

Measuring Cups
