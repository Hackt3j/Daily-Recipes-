Eeg French Fries Recipe:

Potato strips are coated in an egg mixture and deep-fried until golden brown - these egg French fries are super delicious and will make you forget about regular fries.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

10 min.

serving

2

Easy

Tags : Dal Sandwich Method

how to make Egg French Fries

step 1

Peel and cut potatoes into thin strips. Dip in cold water and pat them dry.

step 2

In a separate bowl, crack the eggs, add all-purpose flour, rice flour and the seasonings.

step 3

Pour the egg mixture over the potatoes and mix well.

step 4

Deep fry the coated potatoes and serve hot.

ingredients of Egg French Fries

4 Potatoes

3 Eggs

1/2 cup All Purpose Flour

1/2 cup Rice Flour

to taste Salt

to taste Red Chilli Powder

to taste Oregano
