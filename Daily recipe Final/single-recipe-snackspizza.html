Pizza Baked Potatoes Recipe:

Baked potatoes topped with a delicious pizza filling - this snack brings together the goodness of two dishes in one. Serve these crispy and cheesy pizza baked potatoes as a snack for your next dinner party!

prep time

10 min.

cook time

10 min.

serving

4

Easy

Tags : snacks Method

how to make Pizza Baked Potatoes:

step 1

Start by slicing the potatoes into halves. Bake the potatoes in the oven, make sure they are crisp and brown.

step 2

Meanwhile, heat oil in a pan. Add the chopped onion, tomato, capsicum and garlic and let it cook. Season it with salt, oregano and chilli flakes. The pizza stuffing is ready.

step 3

Add a layer of the cooked pizza stuffing on the baked potatoes. Garnish it with mozzarella cheese.

step 4

Bake prepared potatoes till the cheese melts. The pizza baked potatoes are ready!

ingredients of Pizza Baked Potatoes:

4 Potatoes

1 tbsp Olive oil

1/2 Onion, finely chopped

1 Garlic clove, crushed

1 Capsicum, chopped

400g can Tomato, chopped

1/2 small pack Basil leaves, shredded

to taste Salt

Oregano and chilli flakes (optional)

100g Grated mozzarella
