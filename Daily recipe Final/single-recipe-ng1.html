Chicken Kebabs Recipe

A perfect entree to get your party started! Chicken pieces marinated with a range of spices and shallow fried.

prep time

15 mins.

cook time

30 mins.

serving

4

Medium

Tags : Chicken Prawn Fish

how to make Chicken Kebabs

step 1

1. Mix all the ingredients.

step 2

2. Shape the ingredients into small kababs and refrigerate for an hour or so.

step 3

3. Heat oil and fry the kababs first over high heat, then low, till cooked through.

ingredients of Chicken Dum Biryani

250 gm boneless chicken- minced fine

1 tsp garlic paste

1 tsp ginger paste

1 tsp (green chillies)- chopped to taste

Handful of hara dhania (coriander leaves)- chopped

1 tsp salt or to taste

Oil for frying
