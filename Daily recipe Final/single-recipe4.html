Egg Chapati Recipe:

Egg Chapati Recipe: Egg Chapati is a very quick and easy breakfast recipe made with whole wheat flour, eggs and some vegetables. It is also a good option to pack in kids tiffin.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

10 min.

serving

2

Easy

Tags : Egg Chapati Recipe Method

how to make Egg Chapati

step 1

First of all, take flour in a vessel and knead soft dough with the help of water and keep it aside.

step 2

Break eggs in a bowl, add chopped vegetables and all the spices and beat them well.

step 3

Take a dough from the dough and roll it in the shape of a chapatti.

step 4

Put a little oil on the pan and roast the roti, after roasting from one side, turn the other side and pour some egg mixture on it.

step 5

Sprinkle oil on it and fry the egg side by side, pair the hot chapati with raita or chutney.

ingredients of Egg Chapati Recipe

2 Cups whole wheat flour(tomatoes, carrot, peas, tendli, french beans)

3 eggs

1 small onion, finely chopped

2 green chilli, finely chopped

2 tbsp capsicum, finely chopped

to taste salt

1/4 tsp Red chilli powder

1/4 tsp Black Pepper

1/2 tsp Dhaniya Powder

As per Required Oil

2 tbsp Carrot, grated

tools

Hand Blender

Large Heavy Pot With Lid

Measuring Spoons

Measuring Cups
